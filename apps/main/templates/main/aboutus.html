What makes us different.

Jack Davenport Sweeping Services, Inc. is a premier full service street sweeping, parking lot sweeping, power washing, and day porter service provider in California. Established in 1979, Jack Davenport Sweeping Services, Inc. has been providing top quality service with the industry's most advanced technology for over 40 years.
With over thirty state-of-the-art mechanical broom and regenerative air sweepers in its fleet and twenty-five skilled operators, mechanics, and office personnel on staff, Jack Davenport Sweeping Services, Inc. is the largest, most experienced street and parking lot service provider in the Kern County area and a competitor in other parts of California.
We keep our equipment on a rigorous maintenance schedule to ensure maximum performance. We are sensitive to the environment and have been at the forefront of our industry in acquiring street sweepers that meet the latest environmental regulations and requirements from the California Air Resource Board, including PM10 certification and Rule 1186 AQMD certification.
We currently provide service to over 50 Home Owners Associations containing thousands of homes, as well as various municipalities, school districts, state highways, and retail centers.
If you have a need for street sweeping, parking lot sweeping, porter services, power washing of sidewalks or buildings, or construction or highway cleanup, you have come to the right place! Our operators and equipment will not be outperformed.
