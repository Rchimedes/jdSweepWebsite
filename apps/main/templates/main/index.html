Our Sweepers

Our fleet consists of over 30 broom and regenerative air PM-10 and Rule 1186 certified street sweepers. That means they have the latest technologies to meet EPA and CARB mandates, but can also pick up particles as small as 10 microns per million in diameter - that's about 4-12 times smaller than a human hair!
