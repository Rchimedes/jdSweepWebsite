Environment

We are proud to be able say we...

  • Have a modern fleet of 30 broom and regenerative air PM10 and Rule 1186 certified street sweepers to protect the environment and follow EPA and CARB mandates.
  • Are a California SBE certified contractor.
  • Are proactive in securing the latest technology to protect the environment and service our clients efficiently.
  • Our street sweepers are in compliance with the strict air quality regulations mandated by the California Air Resources Board (CARB).
  • Our PM-10 street sweepers are certified by CARB and the EPA as Tier 4, the highest rating possible.
  • The engines on our sweepers are fitted with components that restrict the emission of noxious gasses. We are currently in the process of upgrading and updating our fleet to meet the latest CARB diesel particulate matter regulations.
  • Jack Davenport Sweeping Services, Inc. is and will continue to be at the forefront of technology and innovation in our industry.
